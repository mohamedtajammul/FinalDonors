DECEPTION 

by FARHANULLAH SHARIFFAUG 20th, 2018

  • Today there is fear, hatred, suffering and pain but no dignity of emotion, or deep or complex sorrows.
  • By the time a funny fidget spinner completes its spin, more than 10 individuals across the globe have their battle against hunger.
  •  How ironic it is, that while we are churning a morsel of food in our mouth, someone out there is breathing his last due to lack of food
  •  We are decieved if we believe that we are incompetent to produce enough amount of food for every human being alive
  •  The world produces enough food to feed twice the world's present population, but food wastage is ironically behind the billions of people who are malnourished.
  • Anually, due to food wastage, India loses $8000,000,000 , U.K loses $15,000,000,000 , Canada loses $31,000,000,000 and U.S.A loses $165,000,000,000.
