what we do!

Yellow bit donors help you donate things like food, clothes, spare e-gadgets, old and working refrigerators, AC's and furniture to the ones in need. 

how we function!

You can either ship the items that you want to donate at our address or you can enter your address and we will pick the items from your place. 
These items will be donated to the needy people in and around Bangalore.
